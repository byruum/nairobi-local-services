Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a tour?

You can book directly through our booking form on the website or contact us via email or WhatsApp.

Do you offer private tours?

Yes. All our tours can be customized and arranged as private experiences.

What is included in the tour price?

Transportation, professional guide services, and listed park or attraction fees unless stated otherwise.

Can I cancel or reschedule?

Yes. Please notify us in advance for rescheduling or cancellation arrangements.

Which payment methods do you accept?

We accept mobile money and bank transfer. Payment details are shared after booking confirmation.
