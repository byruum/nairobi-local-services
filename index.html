Destination Overview – Kenya Highlights

Lake Naivasha

A beautiful freshwater lake in the Great Rift Valley, Lake Naivasha is famous for its peaceful scenery and rich birdlife. Visitors enjoy boat rides, walking safaris on Crescent Island, and close encounters with hippos, giraffes, and zebras. It’s an ideal place for relaxation and nature photography.

Lake Nakuru National Park

Known for its flamingos and large rhino population, Lake Nakuru National Park is one of Kenya’s best places to see endangered black and white rhinos. The park also offers stunning viewpoints, waterfalls, forests, and diverse wildlife including lions, leopards, giraffes, and buffalo.

Masai Mara National Reserve

The Masai Mara is Kenya’s most famous safari destination, renowned for the Great Wildebeest Migration and incredible year-round wildlife viewing. It offers vast open savannahs, big cats, and authentic Maasai culture, making it the ultimate African safari experience.

Nairobi City

Kenya’s vibrant capital city blends modern life with rich culture and wildlife conservation. Highlights include the Giraffe Centre, David Sheldrick Elephant Orphanage, museums, markets, and lively streets. Nairobi is the perfect starting point for any Kenyan adventure.

6-Day Safari Package

Day 1: Pick-up Nairobi → Lake Naivasha

  • Pick-up from Nairobi hotel or airport
  • Drive to Lake Naivasha
  • Boat ride and Crescent Island walking safari
  • Overnight in Lake Naivasha (hotel/lodge)

Day 2: Lake Naivasha → Masai Mara

  • Breakfast
  • Scenic drive to Masai Mara National Reserve
  • Afternoon game drive
  • Overnight at Masai Mara hotel/lodge

Day 3: Masai Mara Safari

  • Full-day game drive with picnic lunch
  • Search for Big Five and other wildlife
  • Overnight in Masai Mara (hotel/lodge)

Day 4: Masai Mara Safari

  • Morning and afternoon game drives
  • Visit Mara River
  • Optional Maasai village visit
  • Overnight in Masai Mara (hotel/lodge)

Day 5: Masai Mara → Nairobi

  • Breakfast
  • Depart Masai Mara
  • Transfer back to Nairobi City
  • Check-in and rest
  • Overnight in Nairobi (hotel/lodge)

Day 6: Nairobi City Tour

  • Nairobi National Park
  • David Sheldrick Elephant Orphanage
  • Giraffe Centre
  • End of tour at hotel / lodge / airport
