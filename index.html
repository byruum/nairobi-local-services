Destination Overview – Kenya Highlights

Lake Naivasha

A beautiful freshwater lake in the Great Rift Valley, Lake Naivasha is famous for its peaceful scenery and rich birdlife. Visitors enjoy boat rides, walking safaris on Crescent Island, and close encounters with hippos, giraffes, and zebras.

Lake Nakuru National Park

Known for its flamingos and large rhino population, Lake Nakuru National Park offers waterfalls, forests, viewpoints and wildlife including lions, leopards, giraffes and buffalo.

Masai Mara National Reserve

Kenya’s most famous safari destination, renowned for the Great Wildebeest Migration, big cats and authentic Maasai culture.

Nairobi City Tour

Visit Nairobi National Park, Giraffe Centre, David Sheldrick Elephant Orphanage, museums and vibrant markets.
