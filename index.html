Destination Highlights

Masai Mara National Reserve

The Masai Mara is Kenya’s most famous safari destination, renowned for the Great Wildebeest Migration and incredible year-round wildlife viewing.

Lake Naivasha

A beautiful freshwater lake in the Great Rift Valley, famous for birdlife, boat rides and Crescent Island walking safaris.

Lake Nakuru National Park

Known for flamingos and large rhino population with stunning viewpoints and diverse wildlife.

Nairobi City

Kenya’s vibrant capital blending modern life, culture and wildlife conservation.

Mount Kenya

Adventure hiking, alpine scenery and unforgettable trekking to Point Lenana.
