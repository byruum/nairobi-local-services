Destination Overview – Kenya Highlights

Discover Kenya’s most breathtaking locations

Lake Naivasha

A beautiful freshwater lake in the Great Rift Valley, famous for boat rides, Crescent Island walking safaris, birdlife, and hippos.

Lake Nakuru National Park

Home to flamingos and endangered rhinos with forests, waterfalls, and rich wildlife.

Masai Mara

Kenya’s most famous reserve, known for the Great Migration, big cats, and Maasai culture.

Nairobi City

A vibrant capital blending wildlife, culture, museums, and modern city life.
