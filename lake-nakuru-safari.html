Lake Nakuru Safari

Home of flamingos, rhinos & breathtaking lake views

Overview

Lake Nakuru National Park is one of Kenya’s most beautiful parks, famous for its flamingos, rhino sanctuary, and diverse birdlife. This safari offers a perfect short wildlife escape with stunning lake scenery and rich ecosystems.

Duration

1 – 2 Days

Highlights

  • Flamingos & over 400 bird species
  • Rhino sanctuary (black & white rhinos)
  • Scenic lake views
  • Guided game drive
  • Professional safari guide

What’s Included

  • Safari vehicle & fuel
  • Professional tour guide
  • Park entry fees
  • Bottled drinking water

What’s Not Included

  • Personal expenses
  • Lunch & drinks
  • Tips & gratuities
